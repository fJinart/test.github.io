식탁이라는 공간에 매료되어 2019년부터는 질감과 색으로 컬러 식탁을 작업하고 있습니다. 작가가 컬러로 정성껏 차린 Color Tables 입니다.
2019년에는 절제된 기하학 형상으로 색감과 질감을 담아내는 Color Tables 작업을 주로 하였고, 2020년 이후부터는 또렷한 형상과 블렌드 느낌이 합쳐진 형태로 Color Tables 작업을 진행하고 있습니다. 캔버스에 담아내는 면 분할과 질감, 색 조화가 주된 관심사입니다. (최신순으로 배열)

The series of Color Tables based on matière and color combination has been proceeding since 2019 after realizing the attraction of the place, “Tables”; I project it on the Color Tables. The Color Tables contained the color appearance and matière as the minimal geometric shapes in 2019; it has been enhanced to apparent and vague shapes since 2020. I’m interested in the harmony of color combination, matière, and surface ratio. The latest is the first below:

The series of Color Plates has been derived from the main series of Color Tables since 2021. The Color Plates are experiments based on color appearance and matière with minimum surface, a triangle. The initial artworks focus on painting with color combinations; the series have been enhanced to other materials such as Korean traditional fabrics like tracing papers, painting including brushing on dyed fabrics like wrinkled papers because I’m interested in paper texture.
